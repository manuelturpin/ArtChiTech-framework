# Skill: Thinking Models

> **"The quality of your thinking determines the quality of your outcomes."**

## Purpose

Thinking Models are structured mental frameworks that improve reasoning, decision-making, and problem-solving. Use `/consider:*` commands to apply them systematically.

## Combining Models

Models work even better in combination:

### For Product Decisions
1. **Pareto** â†’ Identify the 20% of features that matter
2. **Pre-Mortem** â†’ What could kill those features?
3. **Reversibility** â†’ Which are easy to undo if wrong?

### For Architecture Decisions
1. **First Principles** â†’ What do we fundamentally need?
2. **Second-Order** â†’ What are downstream effects?
3. **Circle of Competence** â†’ Do we have expertise for this?

### For Bug Investigation
1. **5 Whys** â†’ Find root cause
2. **Inversion** â†’ How to prevent recurrence?
3. **Map vs Territory** â†’ Is our mental model accurate?

### For Career Decisions
1. **Regret Minimization** â†’ Long-term perspective
2. **Opportunity Cost** â†’ What you're giving up
3. **Reversibility** â†’ Can you go back?
