## Anti-Patterns

⚠️ **Vanity metrics**: Focus on signups instead of engagement.

⚠️ **Ignoring churn**: Acquiring without retaining.

⚠️ **Feature factory**: Adding features without validating usage.

⚠️ **Tech debt explosion**: Ignoring quality for speed.
